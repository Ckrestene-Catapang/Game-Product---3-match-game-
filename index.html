🍬 Candy Crush

Match-3 Puzzle Game

How to Play:

  • Click two adjacent tiles to swap them
  • Match 3 or more tiles of the same color
  • 🎯 Goal: Reach 500 points
  • ⏱️ You have 20 moves

Special Tiles:

  • 💣 Bomb (T-shape): Clears a 3x3 area
  • ⚡ Laser (4 in a row): Clears entire row & column
  • 🍪 Cookie (5 in a row): Clears all tiles of that color
